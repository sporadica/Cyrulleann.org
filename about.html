Copywriting & Digital Design

I am a 26 year old designer, named after the song Cerulean by the Ocean Blue - except my name has the lovely Utah twist. You might remember my shade of blue from The Devil Wears Prada, and cerulean is found in most standard issue crayon boxes. It's a pleasure to meet you!

I have an affinity for a well-punctuated sentence, powerful allegories, and minimalistic graphic design.

I have loved to create since I could form memories, and could often be found writing, illustrating, and stapling together my own picture books in my younger years.

Growing up alongside the internet, I quickly discovered the impact my writing could have on others outside of my family upon entering a contest to win tickets to the Monster Truck Jam. At eight years old, my persuasive email was chosen as the winner of five tickets to the event.

I was ecstatic to find that, with only my writing, I was able to bring my entire family on an outing.

As they say, you'll never forget your first love, and I can confirm this rings true. Try as I might, I will always be a digital design and copy enthusiast, through and through.

In present day, I see to the creation, narration, and presentation of training modules for Marriott Vacation Club Owner Services newcomers, ready to assist with making vacation dreams come true.

In my spare time, I enjoy hiking the various trails of Utah with my four-legged friend, Yuki, as well as exploring the vast wilderness of an open world video game (or several) and collecting insects as a form of real-life Pokémon.
