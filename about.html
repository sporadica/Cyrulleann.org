Copywriting & Digital Design

I am a 26 year old designer, named after the song Cerulean by the Ocean Blue - except my name has the lovely Utah twist. Cerulean is the shade of blue that you might remember from The Devil Wears Prada and found in most standard issue crayon boxes. It's a pleasure to meet you!

I have an affinity for a well-punctuated sentence, powerful allegories, and minimalistic graphic design.

I have loved to create since I could form memories, and could often be found writing, illustrating, and stapling together my own picture books.

Growing up with the introduction of the internet, I quickly discovered the impact my writing could have on others in a contest to win tickets to the Monster Truck Jam, when my persuasive email was chosen as the winner of five tickets to the event. I was ecstatic to find my writing allowed me to bring my whole family on such an outing!

In present day, I oversee the creation, narration, and presentation of training modules for Marriott Vacation Club Owner Services newcomers, ready to assist with making vacation dreams come true.

In my spare time, I like to hike the various trails of Utah, explore the vast wilderness of an open world video game or two, and collect insects as a form of real-life Pokémon.
